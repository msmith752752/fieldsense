# FieldSense Product Vision

## Overview

FieldSense is a field intelligence platform that helps farmers make better planting, irrigation, and harvest decisions using rainfall analysis, weather forecasts, field conditions, and operational risk intelligence.

The platform is designed to help farms make more informed, data-driven operational decisions throughout the entire growing season.

FieldSense is not intended to be just another weather app. The goal is to transform environmental and rainfall data into actionable operational guidance for real farming decisions.

---

## Core Problems FieldSense Solves

Farmers often rely on fragmented information sources when making operational decisions.

Examples include:
- Generic weather forecasts
- Radar apps
- Manual rainfall tracking
- Historical experience
- Delayed field observations

FieldSense aims to centralize and interpret field-level environmental intelligence into operational recommendations.

---

## Core Value Proposition

FieldSense helps farmers answer questions such as:

- Is this field ready for planting?
- Is excessive rainfall likely to delay operations?
- Is irrigation likely needed soon?
- Are field saturation conditions increasing?
- Is a favorable harvest window approaching?
- How do current field conditions compare to historical patterns?

The focus is operational intelligence, not raw weather reporting.

---

## Primary Inputs

FieldSense may eventually support:

- Field location
- Crop type
- Acreage
- Rainfall accumulation
- Weather forecast data
- Soil moisture estimates
- Seasonal weather trends
- Historical rainfall comparisons

---

## Core Operational Intelligence

Potential intelligence layers include:

- Planting readiness analysis
- Rainfall accumulation tracking
- Saturation risk scoring
- Drought condition monitoring
- Moisture trend analysis
- Harvest window forecasting
- Irrigation guidance
- Operational weather alerts

---

## Product Philosophy

FieldSense should remain:

- Clean
- Professional
- Reliable
- Operationally useful
- Data-driven
- Easy to interpret quickly

The platform should prioritize actionable recommendations over excessive complexity.

---

## Architecture Philosophy

FieldSense should be built with a modular architecture from the beginning.

Large files should be avoided whenever possible.

Logic should be separated into:
- Engines
- Routes
- Models
- Services
- Widgets/components

The goal is long-term maintainability and lower risk of large-file corruption or instability.

---

## Not Part of Early MVP

The following ideas are intentionally NOT part of the early MVP:

- Social networking
- Marketplace/e-commerce
- Drone integration
- AI crop disease image analysis
- Complex satellite analytics
- Excessive chart clutter
- Enterprise-only features

The MVP should remain focused on field intelligence and operational decision support.

---

## Long-Term Vision

FieldSense may eventually evolve into a broader agricultural operational intelligence platform capable of supporting:

- Multi-field management
- Seasonal performance analytics
- Advanced forecasting
- Field comparison intelligence
- Risk scoring
- Farm operational planning
- Enterprise agricultural insights

The long-term focus remains helping farms make better operational decisions through field intelligence.
